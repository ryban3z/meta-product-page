Meta Learning Your Way to Mastery

The ability to learn has never been more important. And yet learning effectively is more difficult than ever. Constant distractions, an inability to focus and maintaining momentum are just some of the challenges. No-one ever taught us the habits and strategies that lead to successful lifelong learning, or gave us the tools to rapidly acquire and retain desired knowledge.

The good news is that it is possible to become a more effective and efficient learner by implementing key techniques and principles in a disciplined and concentrated manner. Cognitive science demonstrates that there are optimal ways to learn, you just may never have been taught them, or struggle to implement them.

We call it meta-learning.

Using evidence based techniques, Meta promises to radically transform the way you learn. Meta Learning is an integrated system that enhances the way you learn by improving retention, maintaining focus and optimising the actions you take to understand and recall knowledge.
